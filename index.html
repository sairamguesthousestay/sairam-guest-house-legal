Account & Data Deletion

Application: Sairam Guest House  ·  Package: com.sairam.sairam_guest_house.app

This page explains how users of the Sairam Guest House mobile application can request deletion of their account and the personal data associated with it, in accordance with Google Play's User Data policy.

How to request deletion

To delete your account and associated data, send an email from the address (or phone/account) associated with your Sairam Guest House account to:

sairamguesthousestay@gmail.com

Please use the subject line "Account and Data Deletion Request - Sairam Guest House" and include the phone number registered to your account so we can verify and locate your records.

  1. Email us at the address above from your registered contact.
  2. We verify that the request comes from the account owner.
  3. We permanently delete your account and associated personal data within 30 days of verification.
  4. We send you a confirmation once deletion is complete.

What data is deleted

Upon a verified request, the following data is permanently deleted:

Account & identityName, registered phone number, and login credentials.
Guest ID documentsScanned identity-document images and any text extracted from them.
Contact detailsAddress and any other contact information you provided.
Stay & billing recordsBooking, check-in/out, and payment records linked to your account.

Data that may be retained

We may retain a limited subset of data where required by law (for example, transaction and tax records that must be kept for a statutory period, or information needed to prevent fraud). Any such data is retained only as long as legally required and is then deleted. Retained financial records are kept for the period mandated by applicable Indian accounting and tax regulations.

Contact

For any questions about this process, contact us at sairamguesthousestay@gmail.com.
