Privacy Policy

Application: Sairam Guest House · Package: com.sairam.sairam_guest_house.app

Effective date: 6 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Sairam Guest House mobile application ("the App", "we", "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when it is used to manage guest-house operations. By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information we collect

The App is used by guest-house staff to manage guests and bookings. Depending on how it is used, we may collect:

Account & contact detailsStaff and guest names, phone numbers, and addresses used for login and guest records.
Identity documentsImages of government-issued identity documents captured via the device camera, and text extracted from them (OCR), used to register guests as required for guest-house record-keeping.
Stay & billing dataBooking, check-in/check-out, room, and payment records associated with a guest.
Device & technical dataA push-notification token and basic connectivity/diagnostic information required for the App to function.

2. How we use information

  • To create and manage guest and staff accounts and authenticate users.
  • To register guests and maintain the guest-house records required for operations.
  • To generate invoices and share booking or billing documents at the user's request.
  • To send operational push notifications.
  • To maintain the security and integrity of the service.

3. Camera and permissions

The App requests camera access solely to scan identity documents for guest registration, and notification permission to deliver operational alerts. Permissions are requested only when needed and can be managed at any time in your device settings.

4. How data is stored and shared

Data is stored securely in our backend service (Supabase) and is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS). We use trusted service providers strictly to operate the App:

  • Supabase — secure database and authentication backend.
  • Google Firebase Cloud Messaging — delivery of push notifications.

We do not sell your personal data or share it for advertising. Data is disclosed only to operate the App or where required by law.

5. Data retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide the service and to meet legal, accounting, and tax obligations. When data is no longer required, it is deleted. Certain financial records may be retained for the period mandated by applicable Indian regulations.

6. Your rights & account deletion

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your account and associated personal data. To request deletion, follow the instructions on our Account & Data Deletion page, or email us at the address below.

7. Children's privacy

The App is intended for guest-house staff and business use, and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Security

We use industry-standard measures — including encryption in transit and access controls — to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect your data using reasonable safeguards.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
